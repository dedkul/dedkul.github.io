About Me

Hi, I’m Ashish — a product designer with a background in motion design, and front-end development.


For as long as I’ve remembered, I’ve loved crafting things with my own hands. I got into design because I grew up loving art and code, and wanted to find a way to breath life into static displays and interfaces. I want create a future of products that feels occupied by and made for real people.
